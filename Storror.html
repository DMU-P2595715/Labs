About Parkour

Storror is a well known team of seven athletes who work and create online content about the sport known as parkour. Parkour is a sport of which athletes climb, vault and scale objects and buildings in order to reach a destination in the most effeicient yet technically challenging way possible. Parkour derives from the Frech word Pacours which means route. Storror took this high intensity sport and started uploading messily edited clips of them performing onto youtube all the way back in 2010, however this was when the two founders of Storror were the only members of the team, Benj Cave and Max Cave.

Storror comprises seven athletes including:

  • Max Cave
  • Benj Cave
  • Drew Taylor
  • Toby Segar
  • Callum Powell
  • Sacha Powell
  • Josh Burnett-Blake

As a professional Parkour and Specialist Stunt Performance team they have undertaken work for films, advertising, live performances, and online content creation as they are a self-sufficient film production unit. The team have become notorious for their daring and technical challenges such as jumping across high rise buildings in Hong Kong, Tokyo and Seol to acting as Stunt Men in Michael Bay's film 6 Underground.>/p>
